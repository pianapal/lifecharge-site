Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Life Charge Chiropractic ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit lifechargechiropractic.com or interact with our office.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Information you provide: name, phone, email, and details you submit through our contact form, scheduling tools, or by phone.
  • Health information: only collected during in-office visits, governed by HIPAA and our separate Notice of Privacy Practices.
  • Automatically collected information: IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring site, and similar data via standard server logs and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule appointments
  • To provide and improve patient care
  • To communicate about appointments, care plans, and our services
  • To improve the website and understand how visitors use it
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Cookies & Analytics

This site may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand traffic patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. We do not sell visitor data.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as scheduling, analytics, or hosting platforms) only as needed to operate the practice, and only under confidentiality obligations. We may also disclose information when required by law.

5. HIPAA & Patient Records

Protected health information collected during care is handled under federal HIPAA regulations and our office's Notice of Privacy Practices. Patients receive a copy of that notice on their first visit and can request a copy at any time.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data using industry-standard practices.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children online. Parents and guardians may contact us with any concerns.

8. External Links

Our website may link to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their policies separately.

9. Your Rights

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to legal and clinical-record retention requirements.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
