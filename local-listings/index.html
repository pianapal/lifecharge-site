Why We Maintain Consistent Listings

One office, one set of facts, everywhere.

Search engines and directories cross-check business listings against each other. When the address, phone, or hours match across many places, the engines treat that business as more trustworthy and rank it higher locally.

When listings disagree, it confuses both the algorithms and the patients. Someone who calls the wrong number from an outdated profile may give up and never make it to the office.

If you find a listing for our office somewhere with the wrong information, send us a note at office@lifechargechiropractic.com and we will get it corrected.

Found something out of date?

Email office@lifechargechiropractic.com and we'll get it corrected. Or just come by 1921 Nashville Pike, Gallatin, TN.
