How Evaluation Works

Same process for every condition.

No matter which page brought you here, the first visit follows the same shape. A focused conversation about what is actually going on, a hands-on chiropractic exam, and when the findings call for it, digital X-rays and thermal imaging. You get a report of findings before any care begins, so you know exactly what we found and what the plan is.

Whole-system care means the exam looks at how the spine, the nervous system, and the rest of the body are functioning together. A patient who comes in for headaches often leaves with a clearer picture of how the upper cervical spine is involved. A patient who comes in for sciatica often discovers that the pelvis is where the actual driver lives.

That is the value of evaluating before treating. The condition you booked for is a starting point, not necessarily the full picture.
